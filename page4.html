Ammenities

At our hotel, we pride ourselves on offering world-class amenities that enhance your stay and immerse you in the natural beauty and vibrant culture of Jamaica. From tranquil moments by the sea to unforgettable dining experiences, our amenities are designed to help you relax, indulge, and connect with the essence of the island. Each feature of our hotel reflects our commitment to luxury, comfort, and authentic Jamaican hospitality.

Imagine starting your day on our private beach, where soft sands and crystal-clear waters provide the perfect setting for relaxation or adventure. Whether you want to bask in the sun, take a refreshing swim, or simply enjoy the sound of the waves, our private beach offers a peaceful escape from the world.

As the sun begins to set, there’s no better place to unwind than at our beachfront bar. Savor a refreshing cocktail or a signature rum punch while soaking in the breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea. The beachfront bar is the perfect spot to connect with loved ones, meet new friends, or enjoy a quiet moment by the water.

No visit to Jamaica is complete without indulging in its incredible cuisine, and our authentic dining experiences bring the flavors of the island to your plate. From classic jerk chicken to fresh seafood prepared with local spices, every dish celebrates Jamaica’s rich culinary heritage. Paired with a warm and inviting atmosphere, our dining options promise to delight your senses and leave you craving more.

Private Beach

Enjoy the ultimate tropical experience at our private beach, where soft white sands meet the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean. Perfect for a day of relaxation or water activities, our beach offers exclusive access, allowing you to unwind without the crowds. Lounge under a thatched umbrella, take a swim, or simply enjoy the soothing sounds of the ocean. With beach attendants providing service, you can enjoy chilled beverages and fresh towels right at your spot. Whether you're looking for adventure or tranquility, our private beach promises a perfect escape.

Beachfront Bar

Indulge in island-inspired drinks at our beachfront bar, where every sip is a taste of paradise. Located steps away from the beach, it offers the perfect spot to relax and take in panoramic views of the sea. From signature cocktails to local rum-based drinks, the bar offers a wide variety to suit your preferences. Enjoy the cool ocean breeze as you sip on refreshing beverages, or unwind at sunset with your favorite cocktail in hand. The beachfront bar is the perfect place to socialize or simply enjoy a peaceful moment by the water.

Authentic Dining

Experience true Jamaican flavors at our authentic dining experience, where local ingredients come together to create mouthwatering dishes. From fresh seafood to hearty Jamaican classics, our chefs bring traditional recipes with a modern twist. Indulge in jerk chicken, curried goat, and more, while enjoying the warm, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you’re dining indoors or al fresco with a view of the sea, every meal is a celebration of Jamaica’s culinary heritage. Our authentic dining experience will transport your taste buds and offer a true taste of island life.
