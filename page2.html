Explore Negril

Negril, Jamaica, is a vibrant town known for its stunning beaches, breathtaking cliffs, and rich cultural heritage. While you’re staying with us, we encourage you to explore the beauty and history that make Negril such a unique destination. From its natural wonders to its lively local culture, Negril offers something for every type of traveler.

The spirit of Negril is deeply rooted in Jamaica’s proud heritage and resilience. Learn about the fascinating stories of Jamaica’s Indigenous Taino people, who first inhabited this land, leaving behind a legacy of connection with nature and community. Discover the island’s inspiring path to independence and the strength of its people, who continue to shape its future. Negril is a living testament to Jamaica’s vibrant music and art scenes, where reggae, dancehall, and visual art come to life in every corner.

Embrace the spirit of freedom as you walk along the shores of 7 Mile Beach, where locals and visitors alike enjoy the beauty of the Caribbean Sea. Dive deeper into Jamaican culture with a visit to local markets, where you can find handmade crafts and delicious island delicacies. Explore the area’s natural wonders, from lush rainforests to majestic cliffs, each with its own story to tell.

Negril is also home to traditions and events that highlight the ingenuity and joy of its people. By delving into Negril’s history and culture, you’ll come away with a deeper appreciation for Jamaica’s true heart and soul.

Blue Hole

Discover the magic of Jamaica’s Blue Hole, a hidden gem nestled in the lush hills of Ocho Rios. This pristine paradise boasts cascading waterfalls, crystal-clear natural pools, and rope swings for the adventurous spirit. Perfect for swimming, cliff jumping, or simply soaking in the breathtaking scenery, this guided tour ensures a memorable day in nature. Our hotel provides seamless travel arrangements to and from this tranquil oasis, so you can relax and enjoy the experience worry-free.

7 Mile Beach

Experience the beauty of Negril’s iconic 7 Mile Beach, a stretch of powdery white sand and turquoise waters. Stroll along the shoreline, swim in the warm, gentle sea, or sip on a refreshing drink under swaying palm trees. This tour is perfect for relaxation and soaking up the vibrant island vibes. With round-trip transportation from the hotel, you can focus on enjoying this tropical paradise.

Cliffs and Caves

Explore the captivating Cliffs and Caves of Negril, where stunning views meet rich history. Your guided tour takes you to dramatic limestone cliffs overlooking the Caribbean Sea, perfect for cliff diving or sunset gazing. Discover hidden caves with fascinating formations and stories from Jamaica’s past. Whether you’re an adventurer or a photographer, this tour offers incredible moments to cherish. With hotel-provided transportation, your journey to these awe-inspiring landscapes will be as smooth as the ocean breeze.
