Get To Know Us

Welcome to our hotel, where the beauty of Jamaica meets exceptional hospitality and authentic cultural experiences. Nestled along the stunning coastline of Negril, our hotel was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to create a place where guests can connect with the heart and soul of Jamaica while enjoying world-class comfort and luxury. Every detail of our hotel reflects our deep love for this island and its people, offering an experience that is as warm and inviting as the Jamaican sun.

Our story begins with a vision to showcase the unparalleled beauty and vibrant spirit of Jamaica. From the tranquil beaches to the lush tropical landscapes, our hotel was designed to celebrate everything that makes this island so special. We strive to provide not just a place to stay, but a destination where every moment becomes a cherished memory. Whether you’re here for relaxation, adventure, or a little of both, we are committed to making your visit truly unforgettable.

At the core of our hotel is a dedication to the community and culture of Jamaica. We are proud to employ local staff, incorporate sustainable practices, and feature Jamaican art, cuisine, and traditions throughout our property. By staying with us, you’re not only enjoying a luxurious getaway but also supporting the people and heritage that make Jamaica unique.

Our team is here to ensure your stay is exceptional from start to finish. Whether you’re lounging on our private beach, sipping cocktails at the beachfront bar, or savoring authentic island flavors in our restaurant, you’ll feel the warmth and care that define our hospitality. We invite you to explore all that our hotel and Jamaica have to offer and look forward to welcoming you to our little slice of paradise.

Our Founder

Our founder’s journey began in Jamaica, where he was inspired by the island's vibrant culture and natural beauty. After migrating to the U.S., he worked tirelessly to build a new life while never forgetting his dream of returning to Jamaica. With a vision to share his homeland’s wonders with the world, he saved, learned the hospitality industry, and built the connections necessary to bring his dream to life.

Upon returning to Jamaica, he selected a location that perfectly captured the island’s charm—where turquoise waters meet golden sands and lush greenery. He created a resort not only to showcase the island’s beauty but to provide jobs for the local community and offer an authentic Jamaican experience.

Today, the hotel stands as a tribute to his dedication, bringing the soul of Jamaica to life for visitors from around the world. It’s a place where every corner reflects the founder’s dream, blending tradition with luxury and community.

About Us

At the heart of our hotel is a team of dedicated individuals who are committed to providing exceptional service and creating a welcoming atmosphere for every guest. Our staff, many of whom are local to Jamaica, bring a deep sense of pride and warmth to their roles. They are passionate about sharing the island’s rich culture and traditions, offering a truly authentic Jamaican experience.

Whether you’re greeted with a smile at check-in, served a delicious meal at our restaurant, or assisted with booking a guided tour, our team works tirelessly to ensure your stay is nothing short of exceptional. Their attention to detail, care, and genuine hospitality are what make our hotel feel like home for every guest.

We are proud to have a team that not only provides outstanding service but also contributes to the local community by supporting sustainable practices and creating job opportunities.
